Technical committees

GAME

DEVELOPMENT

The Game Development committee where the fun happens! Using the Unity Engine, Volunteers turn imagination into a real world that players can interact with and experience a thrill that people will never forget.

MACHINE

LEARNING

The Machine learning committee focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn, gradually improving its accuracy. Members are trained to create a model by applying various algorithms to solve real-world problems.

DATA

ANALYSIS

Data Analysis committee
uses Statistics, Visuals, Data Analysis process of Questioning, Wrangling, and Communicating data that helps predicting and understanding the upcoming business events and guide decisions.

MOBILE

APPLICATION

The Mobile Development committee is focusing on developing software for helping mobile users and provides online services through mobile applications with high performance and quality using Flutter Framework

WEB

DEVELOPMENT

The Web Development committee focuses with the volunteers on developing new technical and soft skills to be able build a full stack website.

Non-Technical committees

Graphic

Design

The Graphic design is the visual way of communicating. Members are trained to create this communication by applying various types of styles to meet the visual needs.

Video

Production

The Photography and Video production committee is the studio where creativity meets reality. Their duty is to show the world the team's steps in the form of photos and videos.

Social

Media

The heart of online documenting to everything happens. Their duty is to plan, create, and publish a unique content through different social media channels.

Human

Resources

The HR committee provides much-needed soft and technical skills as our go-to to prepare responsible and active members of society.

Public

Relations

The Public Relation committee uses a wide range of media to build and sustain a good image for the team through planned publicity campaigns and PR activity.

Logistics

The event specialists, responsible for every aspect of event planning. Their main duties include selecting venues, determining the cost, and arranging event services.
